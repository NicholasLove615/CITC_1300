The Fun in Pokemon

Pokemon is based in a world where humans can capture and train creatures to battle each other, as well as become friends with these creatures. There is TV shows and movies about Pokemon, video games, and even a trading card games people all over the world can enjoy. The core of gameplay and stories revolve around trainers discovery and exploring a world full of Pokemon, capturing them, and using them to battle other trainers. With Pokemon aimed towards kids when it was created, it has stuck with many of those kids into adulthood while still providing fun for all through the multiple forms of entertainment. Even with the franchise being over 20 years old it is still bringing in new trainers everyday to enjoy the world of Pokemon.

Storyline

For most the Pokemon games and shows they all have a similiar pathway or lore behind them. Following a young trainer's journey to become the best trainer there was. The way to do that this trainer must defeat eight gym leaders to have the chance to challenge the Pokemon League, which is a set of the toughest trainers there is in the world. While on this journey, a villainous team attempts to not only ruin their plans, but also use pokemon for evil. Just another challange the trainer must face on his path to the Pokemon League and becoming the best there ever was. A major recurring lore element is the vreation of the Pokemon world by the mythical Pokemon Arceus, and the subsequent creation of many other legendary Pokemon.
